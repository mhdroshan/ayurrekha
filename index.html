ABOUT US

Ayur rekha life style wellness clinic is a complete solution finder, for all life style related issues, pertaining to mind and body. we provide authentic ayurveda treatments, sports ayurveda, therapeutic yoga, counselling, neuro linguistic training along with regular yoga sessions for men, women and children.

Ayurveda

We at Ayur rekha stands for pure ayurveda treatments for all life style related diseases like obesity, diabetes, hypertension, all bodily aches and the like. We also provide therapeutic yoga sessions, which is a one on one session, for altering life style, as to prevent the recurrance of the condition as far as possible.

Sports ayurveda

Sport, with its inherent risks involved, leads to injury at one time or another for nearly all participants. Sports ayurveda applies medical knowledge to prevent, recognize, assess, manage, and rehabilitate injuries or illness related to sport, exercise, and recreational activity etc. At Ayur rekha,we intend to provide maximum support to the athletes to recover from their injuries and to maintain their fitness.

Counselling & Neuro-linguistic training

At particular junctures of life, where mental support along with medications is required, Ayur rekha comes in with counseling sessions for :
• Pre-menarche
• Pre –conception
• Post- delivery
• Menopause
There is an alarming rise in the number of children with adhd, learning disability and other behavioural disoders. Ayur rekha makes use of neuro linguistic training along with ayurveda to improve their abilities and skills.

Regular yoga sessions
